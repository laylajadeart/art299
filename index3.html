About Me

Hey, there! my name is Layla Beckner and I am an artist living in southern Maine.

I enjoy multimedia art and work with medias such as pastels (oil and soft), acrylic paint, watercolor, charcoal and graphite, glass, found objects, and my personal favorite, a camera!

Some things I love are cats (I have 5), clothing, makeup, spending time oudoors, and taking photos. I am constantly learning how to edit my photos and new ways to make art.

In 2023, my painting was chosen to be displayed in the Portland Museum of Art for Youth Art Month. A work I wasn't proud of was going to be put in, so on a time crunch, I created a painting I liked much better.

My work has also been displayed in an art show for the Westbrook Women's Club, and in the future, I'd love if my art was displayed elsewhere, too.

I pride myself on the time, effort, thought, and creativity that I put into my artworks. One day, I hope to be a photojournalist, documenting events suchs as protests, social events, and nature. I'd also love to do senior photos, wedding/elopement photography, pet/human portraits, and fashion/magazine photography!
